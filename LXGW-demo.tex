\begin{abstract}
  The \pkg{LXGW} Font Family provides an unprofessional open-source Chinese
  font family, which (will be) included in the \pkg{C\TeX 宏集} as a |fontset|
  option.
\end{abstract}

\noindent
The following lists the Chinese name, English name, filename
(with \file{.ttf} extension), and demo of the fonts.
